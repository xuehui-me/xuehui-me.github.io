About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate in the Synthetic Biology Lab, State Key Laboratory of Plant Trait Design, CEMPS of Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), advised by Prof. Yong Wang. I am also enrolled in a joint PhD program with State Key Laboratory of Bioreactor Engineering, East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST), co-advised by Prof. Yu-Hong Ren.

My research focuses on (1) Synthetic Biology and (2) Metabolic Engineering, leveraging advanced tools such as AI and machine learning.
